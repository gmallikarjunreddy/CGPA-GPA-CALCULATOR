How to Calculate

For GPA Calculation:

  1. Select your grading scale
  2. Click "Calculate GPA"
  3. Add subjects using "Add Subject" button
  4. Enter Subject name, Grade, and Credits
  5. Click "Calculate GPA"

For CGPA Calculation:

  1. Select your grading scale
  2. Click "Calculate CGPA"
  3. Add semesters using "Add Semester" button
  4. Enter Semester name and GPA
  5. Click "Calculate CGPA"

Grade Points Reference:

10-Point Scale:

O=10, A+=9, A=8, B+=7
B=6, C=5, P=4, F=0

5-Point Scale:

A=5, B=4, C=3
D=2, E=1, F=0

Note: Credits should be whole numbers (1-5 typically)
